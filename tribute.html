VIRAT KOHLI

Run Machine - King Kohli



  • Virat Kohli born 5 November 1988 is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the India national team.
  • A right-handed top-order batsman, Kohli is regarded as one of the best contemporary
    batsmen in the world.
  • He plays for Delhi in domestic cricket and for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the
    Indian Premier League (IPL) as captain of the franchise since 2013.
  • Kohli became the fastest cricketer in terms of innings to score 20,000 runs in international cricket,
    doing so in his 417th innings.
  • Kohli is considered as an enterprising batsman, possessing technical abilities and a dynamic playing demeanor
  • In 2013, Kohli established the Virat Kohli Foundation with a philanthropic objective to support underprivileged children
